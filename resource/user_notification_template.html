How to Reset a Forgotten Password

If you forgot your password and need to reset it, follow the instructions below.

  1. Open the Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) site in your web browser.
  2. Enter your username or email, complete the captcha challenge, and click Next..


  3. Choose your preferred verification step and enter the necessary information.


  4. Enter your new password and click Finish.


    You've successfully reset your password.
